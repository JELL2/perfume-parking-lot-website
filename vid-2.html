PERFUME PARKING LOT

PERFUME PARKING LOT is the solo project of musician Joe Ellis.

Providing an outlet with which to release more experimental and electronic compositions, the genre and sound of the project varies greatly.

The project also comprises a visual element inspired Joe's love of movies, heavily exhibited in current releases to date.

The project's first single PLEASE draws from a wide range of influences, with a pulsating 808 beat and shimmering synths.

Combined with a video shot in industrial Digbeth in his hometown of Birmingham, the track aptly demonstrates PPL's ambitions to create an alternative universe of sound and vision.
